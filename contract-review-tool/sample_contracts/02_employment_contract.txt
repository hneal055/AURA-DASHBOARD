EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Employment Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of March 15, 2025, between:

EMPLOYER: Apex Media Group Inc., a California corporation ("Company")
EMPLOYEE: Jordan T. Reeves ("Employee")

1. POSITION AND DUTIES
1.1 Position. Employee is hired as Senior Production Manager, reporting to the Vice President of Operations.

1.2 Duties. Employee shall perform all duties as assigned by Company in its sole discretion. Company reserves the right to change Employee's title, duties, reporting structure, and work location at any time without notice and without constituting a change in terms of employment.

1.3 Exclusive Service. Employee shall devote 100% of their professional time to Company and shall not engage in any outside employment, consulting, or business activity without prior written approval from Company's CEO.

2. COMPENSATION
2.1 Base Salary. Employee shall receive an annual base salary of $78,000, payable in bi-weekly installments, less all applicable taxes and withholdings.

2.2 Bonuses. Any bonus is entirely discretionary and subject to Company's financial performance. No bonus shall be deemed earned or vested unless Employee is actively employed on the date of payment. Company may eliminate the bonus program at any time without notice.

2.3 Benefits. Employee shall be eligible for health benefits after a 90-day waiting period, subject to plan terms and eligibility requirements, which Company may modify or terminate at any time.

2.4 Expenses. Employee shall be reimbursed for pre-approved business expenses only. Reimbursement requests submitted more than 30 days after the expense date will not be reimbursed.

3. AT-WILL EMPLOYMENT
EMPLOYEE'S EMPLOYMENT IS AT-WILL. Either party may terminate the employment relationship at any time, with or without cause, and with or without notice. Nothing in this Agreement alters Employee's at-will status.

4. NON-COMPETE AND NON-SOLICITATION
4.1 Non-Compete. For a period of 24 months following termination of employment, for any reason, Employee shall not directly or indirectly engage in, own, manage, operate, or be employed by any business that competes with Company within the State of California or within a 500-mile radius of any Company office.

4.2 Non-Solicitation. For 24 months post-termination, Employee shall not solicit or hire any Company employee or contractor, or solicit any Company client or customer.

4.3 Enforcement. Employee acknowledges that breach of this Section would cause irreparable harm entitling Company to injunctive relief without bond.

5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
5.1 Assignment. Employee agrees that all work product, inventions, discoveries, developments, and creative works conceived, reduced to practice, or made during employment, whether or not during working hours or using Company resources, are the sole and exclusive property of Company. Employee hereby assigns all such rights to Company.

5.2 Prior Inventions. Employee represents that there are no prior inventions or works to be excluded from this assignment.

5.3 Moral Rights Waiver. To the extent permitted by law, Employee waives all moral rights in all work product.

6. CONFIDENTIALITY
Employee shall not disclose any confidential or proprietary information of Company during or after employment, for a period of 5 years following termination. This obligation survives termination of this Agreement.

7. TERMINATION
7.1 By Company. Company may terminate Employee at any time for any reason. Upon termination without cause, Company shall pay only through the last day worked. No severance shall be due unless set forth in a separate written agreement signed by Company's CEO.

7.2 By Employee. Employee must provide 30 days written notice of resignation. Company may, at its option, waive such notice and terminate immediately upon Employee's notice of resignation, in which case no additional compensation shall be owed.

8. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
All disputes arising from or related to this Agreement or Employee's employment shall be resolved exclusively through binding arbitration administered by JAMS pursuant to its Employment Arbitration Rules. EMPLOYEE WAIVES THE RIGHT TO A JURY TRIAL AND THE RIGHT TO PARTICIPATE IN ANY CLASS OR COLLECTIVE ACTION. Costs of arbitration shall be split equally between the parties.

9. ENTIRE AGREEMENT
This Agreement supersedes all prior agreements, representations, or understandings relating to Employee's employment. No oral modifications shall be binding.
