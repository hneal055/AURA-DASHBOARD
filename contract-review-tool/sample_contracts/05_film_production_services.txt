FILM PRODUCTION SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Film Production Services Agreement ("Agreement") is dated May 1, 2025, and is entered into between:

PRODUCTION COMPANY: Summit Frame Productions Inc., a California corporation ("Production")
SERVICE PROVIDER: Holloway Grip & Lighting Co., a California LLC ("Vendor")

PROJECT: "The Last Signal" — Feature Film (the "Picture")

1. SERVICES
Vendor agrees to provide grip and lighting equipment rental and on-set crew services as detailed in Schedule A attached hereto ("Services"), for the production of the Picture.

2. SERVICE PERIOD
Pre-production prep: May 12–16, 2025
Principal photography: May 19 – July 11, 2025 (estimated 40 shoot days)
Strike/wrap: July 12–14, 2025
Total estimated service period: 10 weeks. Production may extend the shoot schedule by up to 10 additional days with 48 hours' notice; Vendor shall accommodate such extension at the day rates set forth in Schedule A.

3. COMPENSATION
3.1 Equipment Rental. Per Schedule A rates. Estimated total: $187,000.

3.2 Crew Labor. Per Schedule A rates, inclusive of overtime at applicable union or Guild rates. Estimated total: $94,500.

3.3 Total Estimated Contract Value: $281,500. Final amounts subject to actual days worked and equipment utilized.

3.4 Payment Schedule:
- 25% deposit ($70,375) due upon execution
- 25% ($70,375) due on first day of principal photography
- 25% ($70,375) due on day 20 of principal photography
- Final balance due within 30 days of wrap, pending delivery of final invoices

3.5 Late Payment. Amounts outstanding beyond 30 days shall accrue interest at 2% per month. In the event of non-payment, Vendor reserves the right to remove equipment from set upon 24 hours' notice, and Production shall be liable for all resulting production delays.

4. CREDIT
Vendor shall receive the following credit in the Picture: "Grip & Lighting Services: Holloway Grip & Lighting Co." in the end credits, in a size and placement determined solely by Production. No casual or inadvertent failure to provide credit shall constitute a breach of this Agreement.

5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
All footage, materials, and content created in connection with the Picture, incorporating Vendor's equipment or crew services, shall be the sole and exclusive property of Production. Vendor waives all rights in and to any such content. Vendor shall not photograph, film, or record on set without Production's prior written consent.

6. INSURANCE
Vendor shall maintain throughout the service period:
- Commercial General Liability: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $4,000,000 aggregate
- Workers' Compensation: Statutory limits
- Equipment/Inland Marine: Replacement value of all equipment on set
Vendor shall name Production as an additional insured on all policies and provide certificates of insurance prior to commencement of Services.

7. EQUIPMENT RESPONSIBILITY
7.1 Production shall be responsible for loss of or damage to Vendor's equipment caused by Production's negligence, at replacement cost. Normal wear and tear excepted.

7.2 Vendor shall maintain all equipment in good working order. Equipment failures due to Vendor's failure to maintain shall not entitle Production to any day rate reduction for time lost.

8. CONFIDENTIALITY
Vendor shall keep confidential all aspects of the Picture, including story, cast, locations, and budget, and shall not make any public statements or social media posts regarding the Picture without Production's prior written approval.

9. FORCE MAJEURE
Neither party shall be liable for delays caused by circumstances beyond reasonable control, including labor strikes, acts of God, government orders, or pandemic restrictions. Production shall not be obligated to pay for unworked days during a force majeure event.

10. TERMINATION
10.1 Production may terminate this Agreement at any time upon 5 days' written notice. In the event of such termination, Production shall pay only for Services rendered and equipment used through the termination date, plus a kill fee equal to 10% of the remaining unpaid contract value.

10.2 Vendor may terminate only for Production's material breach if uncured within 48 hours of written notice.

11. INDEMNIFICATION
Each party shall indemnify and hold the other harmless from third-party claims arising from the indemnifying party's breach of this Agreement or negligence. Production's indemnification obligations are capped at the total amount paid to Vendor under this Agreement.

12. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
Disputes shall be resolved by binding arbitration before a single arbitrator in Los Angeles, California, under the JAMS Streamlined Arbitration Rules. Judgment on any award may be entered in any court of competent jurisdiction. The non-prevailing party shall pay the prevailing party's reasonable attorneys' fees.

13. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of California.
