ENTERTAINMENT PITCH NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

This Non-Disclosure Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of May 15, 2025, between:

PRODUCTION COMPANY: Ironwood Pictures LLC, a California LLC ("Company")
SUBMITTING PARTY: Tara N. Osei, an individual writer ("Creator")

RECITALS
Creator has developed an original feature film concept titled "Frequencies" (the "Project") and wishes to pitch the Project to Company. Company is willing to hear the pitch subject to the terms of this Agreement.

IMPORTANT NOTICE TO CREATOR: This Agreement protects Company's existing and future development activities. Please read carefully before proceeding.

1. PURPOSE
The purpose of this Agreement is to allow Creator to present the Project to Company's development team while establishing the parties' respective rights and obligations.

2. COMPANY'S EXISTING MATERIAL DISCLAIMER
Creator acknowledges that Company has had, and continues to have, many projects in development. It is possible that Company has received or is developing material similar to the Project from other sources. Creator agrees that Company's development or production of similar material shall not constitute a breach of this Agreement, provided Company does not directly use Creator's specific expression or non-public creative elements.

3. CREATOR'S CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION
For purposes of this Agreement, Creator's Confidential Information means only the specific written materials submitted to Company in connection with the pitch ("Submitted Materials"), clearly marked as confidential at time of submission.

Confidential Information does NOT include:
(a) General themes, genres, loglines, or story concepts;
(b) Any idea, story element, or concept that has been publicly disclosed by Creator;
(c) Material independently developed by Company;
(d) Information Company receives from third parties.

4. COMPANY'S OBLIGATIONS
Company agrees to:
(a) Not reproduce the Submitted Materials for distribution outside of its development team;
(b) Not directly disclose Creator's identity in connection with the Submitted Materials to unaffiliated third parties without Creator's consent;
(c) Consider the Project in good faith.

Company does NOT agree to:
- Keep the pitch meeting or general subject matter confidential;
- Restrict its development of thematically similar projects;
- Provide feedback, response timeline, or option priority.

5. CREATOR'S REPRESENTATIONS
Creator represents and warrants that:
(a) Creator is the sole author and owner of the Submitted Materials;
(b) The Submitted Materials do not infringe any third-party rights;
(c) Creator has not previously disclosed the specific Submitted Materials under any other confidentiality restriction.

6. TERM
Company's obligations under Section 4 shall apply for 18 months from the date of submission. After 18 months, Company shall have no further obligations with respect to the Submitted Materials.

7. SUBMISSION RELEASE
Creator acknowledges that this Agreement does not guarantee Company will option, develop, or produce the Project. Creator agrees not to bring any claim against Company based on the submission of the Project unless Company has directly and substantially incorporated Creator's specific copyrightable expression without authorization.

8. RETURN OF MATERIALS
Upon Creator's written request following a decision not to proceed, Company shall return or destroy the Submitted Materials within 30 business days.

9. NO OBLIGATION
Nothing in this Agreement obligates Company to enter into any further agreement with Creator. Company retains full discretion regarding development decisions.

10. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
Any dispute shall be submitted to binding arbitration under JAMS rules in Los Angeles, California. Each party shall bear its own costs and fees unless the arbitrator finds bad faith conduct.

11. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of California.
