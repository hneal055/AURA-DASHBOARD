RESIDENTIAL LEASE AGREEMENT

This Residential Lease Agreement ("Lease") is entered into as of March 1, 2025, between:

LANDLORD: Pacific Coast Realty Group LLC ("Landlord")
TENANT(S): Amanda R. Flores and Kevin J. Flores ("Tenant")

PROPERTY: 2847 Oleander Drive, Unit 3B, Santa Monica, CA 90403 ("Premises")

1. TERM
This Lease shall commence on March 1, 2025, and shall expire on February 28, 2026 ("Initial Term"). Upon expiration, this Lease shall automatically convert to a month-to-month tenancy under the same terms unless either party provides 60 days' written notice of non-renewal.

2. RENT
2.1 Monthly Rent. Tenant shall pay $3,200 per month, due on the 1st of each month. Rent received after the 3rd shall incur a late fee of $150 per day until paid.

2.2 Rent Increase. Upon conversion to month-to-month, Landlord may increase rent upon 30 days' written notice, with no limitation on the amount of increase.

2.3 Security Deposit. Tenant shall deposit $6,400 (two months' rent) as a security deposit. Landlord may deduct from the deposit for unpaid rent, cleaning, or any damage beyond normal wear and tear, as determined solely by Landlord. Landlord shall return any remaining deposit within 21 days of Tenant vacating.

2.4 First and Last Month. Tenant shall pay first month's rent ($3,200) and last month's rent ($3,200) upon execution, totaling $6,400 prepaid rent.

3. UTILITIES
Tenant shall be solely responsible for all utilities including gas, electricity, water, trash, cable, and internet. Any utility bills charged to Landlord due to Tenant's actions shall be billed to Tenant with a $50 processing fee.

4. OCCUPANTS
Only the named Tenants and their minor children may occupy the Premises. Overnight guests may not stay more than 7 consecutive nights or 14 total nights per calendar month without Landlord's written consent. Violation of this provision shall be grounds for immediate termination.

5. PETS
No pets of any kind are permitted on the Premises, including visiting pets. Any unauthorized pet shall result in a $500 fee per occurrence plus costs to repair any pet-related damage, and shall constitute a material breach of this Lease.

6. MAINTENANCE
6.1 Landlord shall maintain the structural elements, roof, plumbing, and HVAC systems.

6.2 Tenant shall maintain the Premises in a clean and habitable condition, promptly report any damage or needed repairs, and be responsible for all repairs costing less than $200 arising from Tenant's use.

6.3 Tenant shall not alter, paint, or modify the Premises without written consent. All approved modifications shall become Landlord's property.

7. ENTRY BY LANDLORD
Landlord shall provide 24 hours' notice before entering the Premises for non-emergency purposes. In an emergency, Landlord may enter immediately without notice. Tenant shall not change locks without Landlord's prior written consent.

8. SUBLETTING
Tenant shall not sublet the Premises or list it on any short-term rental platform (including Airbnb, VRBO, or similar) without Landlord's express written consent. Unauthorized subletting shall constitute a material breach and grounds for immediate eviction.

9. MOVE-OUT
Tenant shall return the Premises in the same condition as received, normal wear and tear excepted. Tenant shall provide the Premises professionally cleaned, with all carpets steam-cleaned at Tenant's expense. Failure to do so will result in deductions from the security deposit at rates set by Landlord.

10. DEFAULT
10.1 If Tenant fails to pay rent when due or breaches any provision of this Lease, Landlord may serve a 3-day notice to pay or quit (for non-payment) or a 3-day notice to cure or quit (for other breaches).

10.2 If Tenant fails to comply with such notice, Landlord may pursue unlawful detainer proceedings. Tenant shall be liable for all resulting attorneys' fees and court costs.

11. MILITARY CLAUSE
If Tenant is an active duty service member and receives permanent change of station orders or deployment orders of 90+ days, Tenant may terminate this Lease upon 30 days' notice with proof of orders.

12. DISCLOSURES
- Lead Paint: Property was built after 1978; no known lead paint hazard.
- Mold: Tenant shall immediately notify Landlord of any visible mold or moisture issues.
- Registered Sex Offenders: Information available at www.meganslaw.ca.gov.

13. GOVERNING LAW
This Lease shall be governed by the laws of the State of California and applicable Santa Monica municipal codes.
